Can't decide where to eat? Let the wheel decide!

Asian Flavor Spin makes choosing your next meal fun and easy. Spin the roulette wheel to randomly pick an Asian cuisine, browse popular dishes, and discover top-rated restaurants near you with photos, reviews, and ratings from both Yelp and Google.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Spin the wheel to randomly pick a cuisine - Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Vietnamese, or Thai
2. Browse popular dishes for your selected cuisine
3. Discover nearby restaurants sorted by combined Yelp + Google ratings
4. View detailed restaurant info with photos, hours, reviews, and directions

FEATURES:
- Fun roulette wheel to pick your cuisine
- 100 hand-picked popular dishes across 5 Asian cuisines
- Restaurant photos, ratings, reviews, and hours
- Combined Yelp + Google ratings for the most accurate scores
- Map view to see restaurants near you
- Full-screen photo gallery with pinch-to-zoom
- Get directions or call restaurants directly
- Spin again anytime if you want a different pick

Whether you're eating solo, with friends, or on a date night, Asian Flavor Spin takes the stress out of choosing where to eat. Just spin and go!
